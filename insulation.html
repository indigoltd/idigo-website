What is Poly Installation?

Polyethylene vapor barriers are a key step in preventing moisture damage. We install poly sheeting before drywall to stop condensation and mold — especially in basements and exterior walls.

Our team tapes, seals, and staples the barrier to perfection. Combined with insulation, this protects your home from structural decay and air leaks.
